Habit 1 - Be Proactive
Be Proactive: Take responsibility for your life and actions. Instead of reacting to circumstances, be proactive by making choices based on your values and principles.

Habit 2 - Begin with the End in Mind
Begin with the End in Mind: Define your purpose and set clear goals. Visualize what you want to achieve and create a roadmap to guide your actions towards your desired outcomes.

Habit 3 - Put First Things First
Put First Things First: Prioritize your time and energy on activities that align with your goals and values. Focus on important tasks and manage your time effectively to avoid getting caught up in unimportant or urgent but non-essential activities.
